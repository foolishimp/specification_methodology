# Strategy

Pre-constitutional work surface for `specification_methodology`.

This folder holds candidate methodology material under development, review, or deliberation — **not yet constitutional**. Once a strategy artifact stabilises and is ratified, it graduates to `specification/` and becomes constitutional authority.

## Authority boundary

- `specification/` — ratified constitutional material. Live authority. Current-surface writing per `SPEC_METHOD.md` applies.
- `strategy/` — pre-constitutional. Experimental, aspirational, or work-in-progress. Candid about open questions, alternatives, and areas still being shaped.

Downstream consumers must not cite `strategy/` material as authority. Treating strategy material as live specification is drift.

## Graduation path

A strategy artifact becomes constitutional when:

1. The working model stabilises through review and deliberation
2. It is re-authored as a constitutional artifact with the corresponding authority tone, traceability, and present-tense live truth per `WRITING_GUIDE.md`
3. It is placed in the appropriate surface under `specification/` — typically `specification/standards/` for method-level material
4. The `strategy/` copy is withdrawn or explicitly marked as superseded

Until all four steps are done, the material remains pre-constitutional.

## Scope

`strategy/` accepts methodology-layer work across all domains the methodology may grow into:

- organisational patterns (e.g., OODD — Organisation Outcome-Driven Development)
- future domain constitutions beyond world-model and SDLC
- candidate methodology primitives not yet ratified into `SPEC_METHOD` / `ODD_METHOD` / `TICKET_METHOD` etc.
- speculative working models that may or may not graduate

Content that is already constitutional should not live here. Use this folder for work that is explicitly pre-law and under active shaping.
