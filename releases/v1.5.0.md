## Position

`v1.5.0` is the tapped standards compression and ODD execution-authority audit
cut.

This release removes repeated preamble from the shared method and guide
surfaces so each standard opens at its operative law or reference content. It
also ratifies the ODD execution-authority audit rule for GTL/ABG products.

## Material Changes

The standards now remove status, history, governance, scope, relation, and
purpose preambles from the file heads where those blocks duplicated or delayed
the governing truth. `SPEC_METHOD.md` now opens directly at `Canonical
Compression`. Other method and guide files now open at their first operative
section.

`TICKET_METHOD.md` now starts at `Core Model`, preserving the ticket and sprint
law while removing the introductory explanation that repeated the same
authority split.

`WRITING_GUIDE.md` now starts at `Core Rule`, making its own writing rule the
first visible content.

`ODD_METHOD.md` now requires an execution-authority audit for GTL/ABG products
when runtime, start/iterate, worker transport, actor invocation, process
supervision, plugin dispatch, event projection, closure, assurance, retry, or
continuation semantics change. The audit must prove that traversal loops,
actor invocation, process supervision, runtime events, retry/continuation, and
closure folds remain owned by ABG unless the product being changed is ABG
itself or another ratified runtime product.
